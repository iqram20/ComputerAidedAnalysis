## Governing equations (constant ρ, μ)

**Momentum**

$$
\frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t}
+ (\mathbf{u}\cdot\nabla)\mathbf{u}
= -\frac{1}{\rho}\nabla p
+ \nu \nabla^{2}\mathbf{u}
+ \mathbf{f}
$$

**Continuity (mass)**

$$
\nabla\cdot\mathbf{u}=0
$$

**Symbols:**  
$\mathbf{u}$ = velocity,  
$p$ = pressure,  
$\rho$ = density,  
$\mu$ = dynamic viscosity,  
$\nu = \mu/\rho$ = kinematic viscosity,  
$\mathbf{f}$ = body force per unit mass.
